Elephant

The real "kings of the jungle" according to me. Elephants are not only big and scary, but they are also so beautiful to look at. These very large creatures are very shy and are said to have permanent memories.There are two main types of elephants, the African elephant and the Asian elephant. African elephants are larger than the Asian ones, and have ears shaped like the African continent

Fun facts about elephants

  1. African elephants are the largest living land mammals
  2. Their tusks are actually teeth
  3. Their trunks serve many purposes
  4. They can eat up to 150kg of food a day
