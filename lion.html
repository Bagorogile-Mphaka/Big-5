Lion

The kings of the jungle. Did you know that the manes of male lions tell a story? They are not only useful for attracting female lions, but they can also protect their necks during those gruesome fights they encounter with other animals. Sadly though, the lion population has diminished greatly and are on the verge of extinction. Imagine going on safari and not getting to see the great lion himself in all its glory.

Do lions eat people?

Although they don't really like the taste of humans, lions do eat people....if they have to. So stay far away.

Lions are only eaten by a few animals

  1. Hyenas
  2. Cheetahs
  3. Crocodiles and
  4. Wild dogs
