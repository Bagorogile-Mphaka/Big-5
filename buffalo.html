Buffalo

I often refer to the buffalo as a "wild cow", but that is far from the truth. Even though buffaloes and cows are closely related, they are actually different species. Outside of a game reserve, it's really very difficult to run into a buffalo as these are not domestic creatures.Their curved horns help them to fight against predators in the wild.
