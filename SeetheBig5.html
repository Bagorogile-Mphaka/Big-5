See the Big 5

You can find them in almost all the big game reserves all over South Africa, depending on which province you live in. If you are in the Eastern Cape, you can find the Big 5 at the Addo Elephant Park. For those in the Western and Northern Cape, you can travel to the Aquila game reserve to catch the Big 5. The Kruger National park is the the most famous game reserve for seeing the Big 5. For those residing in Limpopo, Mpumalanga, and Gauteng that is the place for you to go. The Madikwe game reserve is the best place to see the Big 5 in the "platinum province" of the North West. Finally, the Thanda game reserve is home to the big 5 in Kwa-Zulu Natal.
